Privacy Policy — Pilgrim Pace

Your Data Stays on Your Device

Pilgrim Pace stores all assessment data, domain scores, pathway progress, and personal settings exclusively in your browser's localStorage. Nothing you enter is transmitted to any server, database, or third party. Clearing your browser data will permanently delete your progress — we have no copy of it.

Information Collected Automatically

Like all websites hosted on GitHub Pages, certain technical information is automatically logged by the hosting provider when you visit the site. This may include your IP address, browser type and version, operating system, date and time of access, and the referring URL. We do not control or access these logs directly.

No Accounts, No Tracking

Pilgrim Pace does not use user accounts, registration forms, cookies, analytics scripts, or advertising networks. We do not track you across sessions or across websites.

External Links

The site contains links to external websites (such as Scripture references and reading resources). We are not responsible for the content or privacy practices of those sites. Visit them at your own discretion.

Data Security

Because all data is stored locally on your device, you control it entirely. We recommend backing up your progress using the export feature if available. We are not liable for data lost due to browser clearing, device failure, or other local factors.

Your Consent

By using this website you consent to this privacy policy. If you have questions, contact: contact@pilgrimpace.com
