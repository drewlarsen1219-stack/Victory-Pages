How to use Victories

Welcome to Victory tracker!

What's this site for?
Track your progress in 12 life skills, get ranked, receive improvement guides, blog your journey, and save your achievements.

How to use:
  1. Click a skill button in the grid.
  2. Fill out the questionnaire - use Next/Previous to navigate multiple pages.
  3. Write about your experience in the blog section.
  4. Upload (photos, documents) if you wish.
  5. Submit & Save will rank you and save your progress.
  6. To save or resume later: Click the "Save" button and bookmark/copy your link; use "Load" to restore from a link.
