Introduction

The Book of "Praises" (Tehillim) תְּהִלִּים

An anthology of hymns and songs containing praises and supplications to God. While many are attributed to David, others were written by inspired scribes and prophets across different eras.
2 Tim 3:16–17, 2 Pet 1:20–21, Isa 40:8

Insights, Notes & Commentary

The Psalters often contain layers of meaning that aren't fully realized until later revelations by other inspired prophets. Because all Scripture is Spirit-inspired and timeless, we can use later prophetic books to illuminate the depth of these ancient songs.
