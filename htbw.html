How to build a text based website

Learning how to build it yourself is the best method, the reason for this is because when I update my website I have to go back into the files to edit them using code.

You can inspect my page to see how I have my pages set up.

I learned how to do it myself and I used Google Gemini for helping me with the more complex stuff like setting up the "paganation" (Next, Previous) buttons.

Most of my website is bloat free, simple and minimilistic as possible while still being useful and aesthetic.

The reason I built it this way because I do not like websites that are too busy with needless flashiness. I am also into the minimilist tech lifestyle which means I simplify my use of technology as much as possible where I can.
